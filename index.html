Stargate Quiz

Do you dare challenge the Goa'uld?

Game Instructions
  1. There are 10 questions to answer, on the subject of the TV series Stargate SG-1.
  2. It is a multiple choice quiz
  3. Once you click on your answer, that answer is final and will go either red(wrong answer) or (green)
  4. If you select the wrong answer, the correct answer will flash green.
  5. You can change the difficulty mode from easy to hard from the main menu.
  6. You can reset the game to the beginning using the reset button.
